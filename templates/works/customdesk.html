007 : Custom Desk

I built this custom solid wood desk to fit in a little nook in our guest room where a standard desk wouldn't fit. The back of the desk easily detaches from the wall and the front legs fold in for storage. The initial concept started as a simple sketch on a piece of paper. Building a 3D model allowed me to make a detailed evaluation of the design before buying any materials. My partner loves her new workspace by the window!
