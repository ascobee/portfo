008 : Backyard Putting Green

I partnered with a friend and fellow engineer to design and build a backyard putting green. Having no prior landscaping experience, we set out to create a 350 sq. ft. artificial grass putting green with 4 holes and two designated chipping areas. Research and planning were critical to creating a realistic putting surface that could withstand the harsh Colorado climate.
